PNC

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC) empowers young Cambodians through quality IT education. Our vibrant student life includes academic excellence, personal growth, sports, canteen life, and cultural activities — creating opportunities to grow both professionally and personally.

PNC VALUES

At the heart of our community are strong values that guide our learning and interactions. We emphasize respect, honesty, responsibility,

and cooperation—helping students not only excel academically but also grow into thoughtful and caring individuals.

Trust

We believe in building mutual confidence through honesty, transparency, and integrity. Trust creates a safe environment where students and staff can grow together.

Solidarity

We support each other, work as a team, and celebrate collective success. Solidarity strengthens our sense of belonging and empowers us to overcome challenges together.

Respect

We value diversity and treat everyone with dignity and fairness. Respect fosters harmony, understanding, and a positive learning community.

Responsibility

We take ownership of our actions, fulfill our commitments, and strive to give our best. Responsibility guides us to be accountable and reliable in everything we do.

AEROBIC

Join our aerobic sessions designed to promote health, energy, and fun. These group workouts combine music and movement to improve

cardiovascular fitness, flexibility, and strength, while also creating a lively and supportive environment for everyone.

Aerobic Training

During school events and celebrations, our Aerobic members bring energy and excitement to the stage. Their performances combine music, movement, and rhythm to create a lively atmosphere that entertains the audience and encourages everyone to stay active. These shows not only highlight their fitness and teamwork but also inspire others to join in and enjoy the fun of aerobic exercise.

Aerobic Show

Aerobic teaching focuses on guiding students through structured exercise routines that combine music, movement, and rhythm. Instructors lead step-by-step sessions, helping participants improve stamina, flexibility, and coordination while enjoying a fun and supportive environment. Through regular practice, students learn discipline, teamwork, and healthy lifestyle habits that go beyond the classroom.

Aerobic Project

Our Aerobic Project brings a modern twist to fitness by combining traditional aerobic movements with the latest TikTok dance trends. Students perform energetic routines that follow popular songs, creating a fun and engaging way to stay healthy. This project not only promotes physical fitness but also encourages creativity, teamwork, and confidence as members showcase their talent through music and dance.
